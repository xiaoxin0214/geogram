Geogram Documentation

Geogram is a programming library with geometric algorithms. It contains the main results in Geometry Processing from the former ALICE Inria project, that is, more than 30 research articles published in ACM SIGGRAPH, ACM Transactions on Graphics, Symposium on Geometry Processing and Eurographics. It was supported by two grants from the European Research Council (ERC): GOODSHAPE and VORPALINE.

Geogram includes a simple yet efficient Mesh data structure (for surfacic and volumetric meshes), exact computer arithmetics (a-la Shewchuck, implemented in GEO::expansion ), a predicate code generator (PCK: the Predicate Construction Kit), standard geometric predicates (orient/insphere), Delaunay triangulation, Voronoi diagram, spatial search data structures, spatial sorting) and less standard ones (more general geometric predicates, intersection between a Voronoi diagram and a triangular or tetrahedral mesh embedded in n dimensions), and semi-discrete optimal transport.
